Mesh Compilation


Entry Fees : ₹ 30/- (Group of 2)

A participant (blind-folded) has to move left, right, forward, backward according to the Morse code provided by their partner. The moving participant should find different PC components kits in the mesh. Then finally they will be given a display structure of an assembled PC out of the case. One of the member will have to observe the connections for an allotted time and direct his partner to make the connections accordingly with the parts they found in the mesh.

Event Incharge :Aditya Pujari, Onkar Nagarkar
Event Heads :Krunal Lad, Pratik Gawad, Rahul Panga

About Us

CSI-VIVA TECH is the student branch of the Computer Society of India (CSI) at VIVA Institute of Technology, Mumbai. The Computer Society of India is a non-profit professional meet to exchange views, information, to learn and share ideas. We offer a range of services and networking opportunities through workshop , seminars and guest lectures for the development of students. We even organise technical fest " TechChase" in collaboration with other technical committee. Our aim is to provide a training ground for computer enthusiasts of tomorrow.
